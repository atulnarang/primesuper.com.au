Keep your super in one spot

Already retired?

If you've already retired but you haven't yet established an income stream product with us, it's important to rollover your external super now – you can't do this once you start the income stream.

Semi-retired?

If you are only semi-retired, you can roll over external super into the super component of your account at any time. However it's always best to rollover sooner rather than later to avoid multiple fees and insurance premiums!

Next steps

To rollover external super into your Prime Super account, simply use our Fetch my super tool which will identify any other super accounts you may have elsewhere.

We'll then help you transfer all of your money into your Prime Super account.

If you can't access the tool online, download and fill out the Fetch my super form below and send it to Prime Super.

Fetch my super form

New employer?

If you're still working and you recently moved to a new employer, they could have already established a new account with another super fund on your behalf. Avoid multiple fees and insurance premiums - rollover this amount today (see above)!
