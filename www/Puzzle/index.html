Step 1

Register & login to MemberOnline

If you haven't registered yet - go to the Login page and click on Register now to get started

Step 2

Update mobile and email details

In the Member section click on Personal details and you can update your details here.

Step 3

Fetch my super

In the Member section click on Personal details and you can give us permission to fetch your super here.

Step 4

Review your super balance

Click on Your Account, have a look at your balance and let us know if you are happy with it.

Step 5

Provide your TFN

In the Member section click on Personal details to confirm if we have your TFN. If we don’t have your TFN you can submit it online.

Step 6

Review your investments

Click on the Investments section and let us know if you are happy with your investment strategy..

Step 7

Register to receive online statements

In the Member section click on Personal details and you can choose to receive an eStatement here.

Step 8

Review your insurance

Click on the Insurance Details section and let us know if you are happy with your insurance cover.

Step 9

Nominate some beneficiaries

In the Member section click on Personal details and you can nominate a preferred beneficiary here.
