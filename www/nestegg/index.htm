Option 1

Employer contributions

Ask your employer to contribute into your Prime Super account

Your employer must pay regular super contributions on your behalf (if you qualify). This is a minimum of 9.5% of your salary.

It's easy to ask your employer to make these contributions into your Prime Super account.

Simply download and fill out the forms opposite and give these to your employer.

This will see your account re-activated so it will no longer be at risk of being transferred to the ATO.

Option 2

Update your details

There are some very simple ways in which you can convert your account from inactive to active – and avoid having your super transferred to the ATO.

  1. Update your contact details – for example your name, address, DOB, phone numbers or email address.
  2. Nominate a beneficiary.
  3. Make an investment choice.

You can do any of these via the secure section of our website or by calling us on 1800 675 839.

If you don’t have secure site access, register via the login page.

Alternatively, simply download and fill out the forms opposite and send them to Prime Super.

You can even go one step further…

…and ask Prime Super to flag on your account that you don’t want your super to be transferred to the ATO – even if your account should become inactive again for some reason.

Simply download and fill out the form opposite and send it in to Prime Super. This nomination will remain valid for a period of five years.

Option 3

Transact on your account

Avoid your account being transferred to the ATO by boosting your super savings above the $2,000 limit. There are two easy options:

  1. Make a voluntary contribution yourself

    This can be a one-off payment or a series of regular payments – whatever you can afford!

    There are two ways to make an after-tax contribution:

    1. Go online – Log in to the secure section to find out how to make a contribution into your account via BPay or EFT.

      If you don’t have secure site access, simply register via the login page.

    2. Fill out a form - download and fill out the Personal contributions form opposite and send it back to us.

      If you’d like to make a contribution before-tax, you’ll need to speak to your HR or payroll representative.

  2. Roll all of your funds into your Prime Super account

    We can find your funds for you and combine them into one account with Prime Super! Find out how

    Alternatively, if you’d like to organise your own rollovers, simply download and fill out the Rollover form opposite and send it back to us. Please note: you need a separate form for each account you’d like to roll into Prime Super.
